Popular Flowers

Roses are popular for their beautiful form and color, their strong association with love and romance, their historical significance, and their ability to be shipped and enjoyed worldwide

Tulips are popular due to a combination of factors including their vibrant colors, symbolic meanings, and historical significance, particularly in the Netherlands, where they are a national icon.

Peonies are popular due to their large, showy blooms, and romantic appeal. They are also known for their fragrance, longevity as cut flowers, and low-maintenance nature, making them a favorite among gardeners and wedding planners.

Daisies are popular due to their widespread symbolism of innocence, purity, and joy, along with their ease of cultivation and low maintenance requirements. They are also known for their versatility in both floral arrangements and garden design.
